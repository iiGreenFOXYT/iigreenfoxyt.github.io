|Best Programing Environment|

The best programming environment does not mean that you have your own laptop and sit in a quiet and comfortable place with a cup of tea or even your coffee, and play your favorite music. I am talking about the operating system that you use on your device, which is the Windows system. Windows 11 was recently released, which has wonderful and useful features, and at the same time, in all versions of Windows there are defects and errors, and sometimes the Windows system becomes a very heavy system on your device, and this affects the programs that you use in programming, such as visual studio code, for example, where You face a problem of slow running and running the plugins inside it that you downloaded, getting incomprehensible errors, or that there are some missing things that visual studio code needs such as node.js and java
And other programming languages are not installed on the Windows system
The question is: If Windows is not the ideal environment for programming, then what is the most suitable environment for that work?
The answer, dear reader, is the Linux systems
Linux systems are slightly complex systems, but they are lighter on your computer
It is easy to use and most programming languages provide you with a ready-made package to support your programming work
One of its advantages is that you can transfer your very large files within a short period of time. For example, you have a partition full of data of 60 GB. You can transfer it to another partition within an hour on a normal hard drive. "I recommend that you use Linux Mint, which is light and easy to use, less than 3 GB."
You can control your server through the command panel in the system, which is "Terminal". Most Adobe programs can be installed for free, as well as Office programs!
